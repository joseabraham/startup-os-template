# Transfer Pricing Documentation for International Sales
## Enterprise Documentation SaaS - Panama S.A.

### Executive Summary

This document establishes transfer pricing policies and documentation requirements for our enterprise SaaS business operating in Panama with international sales activities. The framework ensures compliance with Panama's transfer pricing regulations while optimizing our territorial tax benefits and supporting sustainable international expansion.

### Business Structure & Transfer Pricing Strategy

#### Current Corporate Structure

```
RECOMMENDED STRUCTURE FOR INTERNATIONAL SAAS

Panama HoldCo S.A. (IP Owner)
├── Owns all intellectual property and technology
├── Licensed under CATI regime (if approved)
├── Contracts directly with international customers
├── Receives foreign-source revenue (tax-free under territorial system)
└── Licenses IP to operating subsidiaries

Panama OpCo S.A. (Service Provider)
├── Employs all technical and operational staff
├── Provides services to HoldCo under service agreement
├── Charges cost + reasonable markup (10-15%)
├── Has substance: real operations and employees
└── Pays Panama tax on markup only

International Subsidiaries (Future)
├── Local market presence and customer support
├── License IP from Panama HoldCo
├── Pay arms-length royalties to HoldCo
└── Subject to local country tax rates
```

#### Transfer Pricing Rationale

**Economic Substance Allocation**:
- **IP Development**: All conducted in Panama (R&D team)
- **Technology Risk**: Assumed by Panama HoldCo
- **Market Risk**: Shared between HoldCo (product) and subsidiaries (local market)
- **Operational Risk**: Managed by Panama OpCo

**Value Creation Analysis**:
- **Primary Value**: Created through IP development in Panama
- **Secondary Value**: Enhanced through local market development
- **Cost Savings**: Achieved through operational efficiency in Panama

### Intercompany Service Agreement Framework

#### HoldCo ↔ OpCo Service Agreement

**Service Description**:
Panama OpCo provides comprehensive operational services to Panama HoldCo including:

1. **Technology Development Services**
   - Software development and engineering
   - Product design and user experience
   - Quality assurance and testing
   - DevOps and infrastructure management
   - Security and compliance implementation

2. **Customer Support Services**
   - Technical customer support
   - Implementation and onboarding services
   - Training and documentation
   - Customer success management
   - Bug fixes and maintenance

3. **Administrative Services**
   - Human resources management
   - Finance and accounting support
   - Legal and compliance support
   - Office administration
   - IT infrastructure management

**Pricing Methodology**: **Cost Plus Method**
- **Base Cost**: All direct and indirect costs incurred by OpCo
- **Markup**: 10-15% markup on total costs
- **Benchmarking**: Supported by comparable service provider analysis

**Cost Components**:
```
DIRECT COSTS (100% allocable):
- Employee salaries and benefits
- Payroll taxes and CSS contributions
- Direct technology costs (development tools, licenses)
- Office space and facilities
- Professional services (legal, accounting)

INDIRECT COSTS (Allocated proportionally):
- Executive management time
- Shared services (HR, Finance, IT)
- General office expenses
- Insurance and other overhead
```

**Monthly Billing Process**:
1. OpCo calculates monthly costs by category
2. Applies agreed markup percentage
3. Issues invoice to HoldCo by month-end
4. HoldCo pays within 30 days
5. Both entities maintain detailed cost records

### Compliance Requirements

#### Panama Transfer Pricing Obligations

**Applicability Threshold**:
- **$1.5 million** in related party transactions annually
- Includes services, royalties, loans, and other arrangements
- Aggregated across all related party transactions

**Filing Requirements**:
- **Form 930**: Filed with annual tax return
- **Master File**: High-level organizational information
- **Local File**: Detailed local entity information
- **Economic Analysis**: Transfer pricing study

**Penalties for Non-Compliance**:
- **150% of additional tax** assessed
- **Criminal penalties** for significant understatement
- **Interest charges** on unpaid tax
- **Audit risk** increases significantly

#### Documentation Standards

**Record Retention**:
- **5 years** minimum retention period
- **Digital and physical** copies acceptable
- **Organized filing system** for audit readiness
- **Backup procedures** for critical documents

**Language Requirements**:
- **Spanish translation** for Panama tax authority
- **English originals** with certified translations
- **Consistent terminology** across all documents

- **Mitigation**: Maintain detailed activity logs, decision-making evidence

**Pricing Methodology**:

- **Mitigation**: Annual benchmarking studies, comparable analysis updates

**Profit Attribution**:
