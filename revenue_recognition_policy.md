# Revenue Recognition Policy
## Enterprise Documentation SaaS - Panama S.A.

### Policy Overview
This policy establishes revenue recognition procedures for our enterprise SaaS business in compliance with ASC 606 / IFRS 15 and Panama's territorial tax requirements.

### Accounting Standards
- **Primary Standard**: IFRS 15 - Revenue from Contracts with Customers
- **Alternative**: ASC 606 (if US GAAP reporting required)
- **Panama Compliance**: Territorial tax source determination

### Revenue Streams & Recognition

#### 1. SaaS Subscription Revenue

**Performance Obligation**: Continuous access to documentation platform over subscription period

**Recognition Pattern**: **Over Time** (ratably over contract term)

**Timing**: Monthly as service is provided

**Journal Entry (Monthly)**:
```
Dr. Cash/Accounts Receivable    $10,000
  Cr. Deferred Revenue                   $10,000

Dr. Deferred Revenue           $833.33
  Cr. Subscription Revenue              $833.33
```

**Multi-Year Contracts**:
- Year 1: Current portion of deferred revenue
- Years 2+: Non-current deferred revenue
- Recognize monthly over entire contract term

**Contract Modifications**:
- Treat as separate contract if distinct goods/services
- Modify existing contract if not distinct
- Prospective adjustment to remaining performance obligations

#### 2. Professional Services Revenue

**Implementation Services**:
- **Performance Obligation**: System setup and configuration
- **Recognition**: Over time using input method (hours incurred)
- **Pattern**: Progress toward completion

**Training Services**:
- **Performance Obligation**: User training delivery
- **Recognition**: Point in time when training completed
- **Pattern**: Upon completion of training sessions

**Ongoing Consulting**:
- **Performance Obligation**: Advisory services
- **Recognition**: Over time as services provided
- **Pattern**: Monthly/hourly as performed

#### 3. Contract Assets & Liabilities

**Deferred Revenue (Contract Liability)**:
- Cash received but service not yet provided
- Classify current vs non-current based on 12-month performance period

**Contract Assets (Unbilled Revenue)**:
- Service provided but not yet invoiced
- Typically for time-based professional services

### Panama Territorial Tax Considerations

#### Source Determination Rules

**Panama Source Revenue (Taxable at 25%)**:
- Services performed in Panama to Panama customers
- Software used primarily within Panama territory
- Implementation services performed in Panama

**Foreign Source Revenue (Tax-Free)**:
- Services delivered to customers outside Panama
- Cloud-hosted software accessed from outside Panama
- Remote implementation and training services
- International customer contracts

#### Documentation Requirements

For each revenue transaction, maintain:
1. **Customer Contract** showing:
   - Customer legal entity and address
   - Service delivery location
   - Payment terms and currency

2. **Service Delivery Evidence**:
   - System logs showing user access locations
   - Training delivery location/method (virtual/in-person)
   - Implementation work location

3. **Payment Records**:
   - Bank accounts used for payment
   - Payment currency and method
   - Wire transfer details showing origin

#### Revenue Allocation by Source

**Monthly Tracking Required**:
- Separate GL accounts for Panama vs Foreign source
- Customer-level source classification
- Geographic revenue reporting
- Transfer pricing documentation for related parties

### Specific Contract Types

#### 1. Annual Subscription Contract

**Contract Terms**: $120,000 annual subscription, paid quarterly

**Revenue Recognition**:
```
Month 1-3 (Q1):
Dr. Cash                      $30,000
  Cr. Deferred Revenue               $30,000

Monthly (12 months):
Dr. Deferred Revenue         $10,000
  Cr. Subscription Revenue           $10,000
```

#### 2. Multi-Year Enterprise Deal

**Contract Terms**: 3-year deal, $500k total ($150k Year 1, $175k Year 2, $175k Year 3)

**Performance Obligations**:
1. Platform access (over time)
2. Implementation services ($50k)
3. Training services ($25k)

**Allocation**:
- Platform: $425k over 36 months = $11,806/month
- Implementation: $50k over 6 months = $8,333/month
- Training: $25k when completed

#### 3. Freemium to Paid Conversion

**Recognition**: Only paid subscription amounts
**Free tier**: No revenue recognition (marketing cost)
**Upgrade**: Recognize from upgrade date forward

### Contract Review Process

#### New Contract Approval
1. **Sales Team** negotiates terms
2. **Legal Review** for non-standard terms
3. **Revenue Team** reviews for accounting treatment
4. **Finance Approval** for payment terms
5. **Source Classification** for tax purposes

#### Monthly Revenue Close
1. Review all new contracts signed
2. Calculate monthly revenue recognition
3. Update deferred revenue balances
4. Verify source classification
5. Generate revenue reports by source
