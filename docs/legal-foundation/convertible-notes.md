# Convertible Notes Framework for Panama

:::tip **Flexible Fundraising Instrument**
Convertible notes provide a fast, cost-effective way to raise capital for DocuHarmonize while deferring valuation discussions to a future equity round. This Panama-adapted framework incorporates international best practices with local legal requirements.
:::

## ðŸ“Š Convertible Note Overview

### Why Convertible Notes for DocuHarmonize

#### Strategic Advantages
1. **Speed**: Faster execution than equity rounds (2-4 weeks vs. 3-6 months)
2. **Cost Efficiency**: Lower legal costs ($10-20K vs. $50-100K for equity)
3. **Valuation Deferral**: Avoid early valuation negotiations
4. **Investor Familiarity**: Standard structure recognized by international investors
5. **Flexibility**: Can accommodate various investor sizes and terms

#### Panama Legal Considerations
- **Recognition**: Convertible instruments recognized under Panama commercial law
- **Documentation**: Proper drafting ensures enforceability
- **Tax Treatment**: Debt instrument until conversion (favorable treatment)
- **Registration**: No securities registration required for private placements

**Standard Term**: 18-24 months from issuance
**DocuHarmonize Term**: 24 months
**Rationale**:
- Sufficient time to achieve Series A milestones
- Provides flexibility for market timing
- Standard expectation for seed-stage companies

### Conversion Mechanics

#### Automatic Conversion Triggers
1. **Qualified Financing** (Primary Conversion)
   - **Definition**: Series A preferred stock round â‰¥$3M with majority investor approval
   - **Conversion Price**: Discounted price or valuation cap, whichever is more favorable
   - **Process**: Automatic conversion at Series A closing

2. **Change of Control** (Secondary Conversion)
   - **Definition**: Acquisition, merger, or asset sale >50% of company
   - **Conversion**: Convert to equity immediately prior to transaction
   - **Liquidity**: Participate in transaction proceeds as if equity holder

3. **Maturity Date** (Default Conversion)
   - **Option**: Investor choice to convert at fair market value or demand repayment
   - **Typical Outcome**: Conversion preferred to preserve ongoing relationship

#### Conversion Price Protection

##### Discount Rate
**Standard Range**: 15-25% discount to Series A price
**DocuHarmonize Discount**: 20%
**Calculation**: If Series A price is $1.00/share, convertible holders pay $0.80/share

```markdown

Series A Price: $2.00 per share
```

##### Valuation Cap
**Purpose**: Protects investors from excessive dilution if company value increases significantly
**DocuHarmonize Cap**: $15M pre-money valuation
**Market Positioning**: Conservative cap reflecting early-stage risk

```markdown
VALUATION CAP EXAMPLE

Scenario: Series A at $30M pre-money valuation
Series A Price: $3.00 per share
Cap-Based Price: $1.50 per share ($15M cap = 50% discount)

Investor Benefits from Cap (more favorable):
$100,000 investment = 66,667 shares at $1.50
vs. 41,667 shares at $2.40 (60% more shares from cap protection)
```

##### Most Favored Nation (MFN) Clause
**Protection**: If subsequent convertible notes have better terms, existing holders automatically receive same benefits
**Standard Practice**: Included in all professional convertible note rounds
**Investor Protection**: Ensures early investors aren't penalized for timing

- Anti-money laundering (AML) verification
- GDPR data protection for investor information

#### Latin American Investors
**Advantages**: Familiar with Panama legal system
**Considerations**:
- Currency controls and conversion procedures
- Tax treaty benefits and withholding optimization
- Local investment regulations compliance

### Tax Implications

#### For the Company (Panama)
- **Interest Payments**: Tax-deductible business expense
- **Conversion**: No taxable event (debt-to-equity conversion)
- **CATI Benefits**: R&D deductions may offset interest income taxation

#### For Investors
- **Interest Income**: Taxable as ordinary income in investor's jurisdiction
- **Conversion Gain**: Capital gains treatment upon equity sale
- **Tax Treaty Benefits**: Potential withholding tax reduction
