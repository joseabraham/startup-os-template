# Employment Contracts & Labor Framework

:::tip **Panama Labor Law for Tech Startups**
Panama's labor laws provide flexibility for tech companies while protecting employee rights. This framework enables DocuHarmonize to build a world-class team while maintaining compliance with local regulations and supporting equity compensation plans.
:::

- Initial grant upon hire: [Number] shares of common stock

- Vesting schedule: 4 years, 25% after 1 year, then monthly
- Post-termination exercise period: 90 days

**Equity Participation Terms**:
- Participation in Employee Stock Option Plan (ESOP)
- Anti-dilution protection for standard corporate events
- Information rights for company performance and financing
- Right of first refusal on share transfers

#### Article 4: Work Schedule & Location
**Standard Schedule**:
- 40 hours per week, flexible core hours 10am-4pm (Panama time)
- Remote work: Up to 3 days per week
- On-site collaboration: Minimum 2 days per week
- Flexible scheduling for global customer support

**Overtime Policy**:
- Exempt employee status (no overtime pay for standard hours)
- Compensatory time off for extended work periods
- Additional compensation for emergency weekend work

#### Article 5: Confidentiality & IP Assignment
**Confidential Information**:
- All proprietary technology, customer data, and business information
- Non-disclosure obligations continue post-employment
- Specific protection for AI models and training data

**Intellectual Property Assignment**:
- All work-related inventions assigned to company
- Personal projects exempt if no company resources used
- Obligation to disclose potentially conflicting inventions

#### Article 6: Termination Provisions
**Termination by Company**:
- 30 days notice for termination without cause
- Immediate termination for cause (misconduct, breach of contract)
- Severance: 1 month salary + accrued vacation

**Termination by Employee**:
- 30 days notice required
- Good faith transition of responsibilities
- Stock option vesting acceleration in certain circumstances

**Post-Employment Restrictions**:
- 12-month non-compete for direct competitors (Panama only)
- 24-month non-solicitation of employees and customers
- Return of all company property and confidential information

- Secure handling of HR and payroll data

**Customer Data Protection**:
- GDPR and data privacy training for all employees
- Access controls and need-to-know basis
- Incident reporting and breach procedures
- Regular security awareness training

#### Performance Management
**Performance Review Cycle**:
- Quarterly check-ins and goal setting
- Annual comprehensive performance reviews
- 360-degree feedback for leadership roles
- Career development planning and mentoring

- Support and resources for skill development

#### Mandatory Benefits (Panama Law)
**Social Security (Seguro Social)**:
- Employee contribution: 9.75% of salary
- Employer contribution: 12.25% of salary
- Coverage: Healthcare, disability, pension

**Additional Mandatory Benefits**:
- 13th Month Bonus (D√©cimo Tercer Mes): 1/12 of annual salary
- Vacation: 30 days annually after 1 year of service
- Sick Leave: 18 days annually with medical certification

- Annual health checkups

**Work-Life Balance**:

**Financial Security**:
