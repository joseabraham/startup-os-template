## Equity Compensation

### Stock Option Program

DocuHarmonize believes that every employee should have the opportunity to participate in the company's success through equity ownership.

#### Eligibility and Grants
- **All employees**: Options granted after 90-day probationary period
- **Grant sizes**: Based on role, level, and performance
- **Refresh grants**: Annual grants based on performance and retention
- **New hire grants**: Market-competitive initial grants

#### Vesting Schedule
- **Standard vesting**: 4-year vesting with 1-year cliff
- **Cliff vesting**: No options vest until 12 months of employment
- **Accelerated vesting**: Partial acceleration on acquisition or IPO
- **Departure rules**: Vested options exercisable for 90 days post-departure

### Understanding Your Options

#### Key Terms
- **Strike price**: Price to purchase shares (set at grant date fair market value)
- **Vesting**: Earning the right to exercise options over time
- **Exercise**: Actually purchasing the shares
- **Fair market value**: Current estimated value of company shares

#### Tax Implications
- **Exercise tax**: May trigger income tax on spread (FMV - strike price)
- **Sale tax**: Capital gains tax when shares are sold
- **Tax planning**: Consult with tax advisor for personal situation
- **409A valuation**: Annual third-party valuation sets strike price

### Equity Education

#### Financial Literacy
- **Quarterly equity sessions**: Education about stock options and valuation
- **One-on-one consultations**: HR meetings to explain personal grants
- **Tax planning resources**: Connections to qualified tax professionals
- **Exercise planning**: Tools and guidance for optimal timing

#### Company Performance
- **Regular updates**: Quarterly business reviews include financial metrics
- **Transparency**: Open sharing of key business indicators
- **Growth trajectory**: Clear communication about company milestones
- **Exit scenarios**: Education about different liquidity events
