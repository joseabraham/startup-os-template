## Our HR Philosophy

At DocuHarmonize, we believe that our people are our greatest asset. Our HR practices are designed to:

- **Attract Top Talent**: Compete globally for the best minds in enterprise SaaS
- **Ensure Compliance**: Full adherence to Panama labor laws and international standards
- **Support Growth**: Scalable systems that work from 6 to 500+ employees
- **Drive Performance**: Clear expectations, regular feedback, and meaningful rewards
- **Build Culture**: Remote-first, inclusive, and innovation-focused environment

## Panama Labor Law Compliance

All our HR policies comply with:
- **Código de Trabajo de Panamá**: Full compliance with labor code
- **CSS Requirements**: Social Security contributions and benefits
- **MITRADEL Regulations**: Ministry of Labor requirements
- **Tax Optimization**: Leveraging Panama's territorial tax system
