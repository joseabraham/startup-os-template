## Executive Summary

**Company Profile:**
- Sector: B2B SaaS (Enterprise Documentation & AI/ML)
- Target Market: Enterprise companies (500+ employees)
- Business Model: Multi-tenant SaaS with usage-based pricing
- Geographic Focus: US/LatAm expansion
- AI/ML Core: Document processing, standardization, compliance automation

**Investment Thesis:**
- Large TAM: $15B+ enterprise content management market
- AI-driven defensibility through proprietary data and models
- Strong unit economics potential in enterprise segment
- Significant switching costs once integrated
- Regulatory tailwinds (compliance, governance requirements)

## Pre-Seed & Seed Strategy

### Pre-Seed Round ($500K-$750K)

**Timeline:** Months 1-6

**Use of Funds:**
- Product Development (40%): $200K-$300K
  - AI/ML model training and optimization
  - Core platform development
  - Initial integrations (Confluence, SharePoint, Notion)
- Team Building (35%): $175K-$262K
  - Senior AI/ML engineer
  - Enterprise sales specialist
  - Customer success manager
- Market Validation (15%): $75K-$112K
  - Pilot customer acquisition
  - Product-market fit validation
- Operations (10%): $50K-$75K

**Key Milestones:**
- ✅ 3-5 pilot customers using product
- ✅ Core AI standardization engine functional
- ✅ $5K-$15K MRR from early customers
- ✅ 1-2 enterprise integrations live
- ✅ Team of 4-6 people

### Seed Round ($1.5M-$2M)

**Timeline:** Months 7-18

**Key Milestones for Seed:**
- $50K-$100K MRR
- 20-30 paying enterprise customers
- \<5% monthly churn rate
- LTV:CAC > 3:1
- Product-market fit validation
- Team of 12-15 people

## Series A Preparation

### Series A Readiness Criteria

**Financial Metrics:**
- $1M+ ARR (ideally $1.5M+)
- 15%+ month-over-month growth
- \<3% monthly churn rate
- LTV:CAC ratio > 4:1
- Gross margin > 75%
- Net revenue retention > 110%
- Rule of 40 > 40%

**Business Metrics:**
- 50+ enterprise customers
- Average contract value (ACV) > $25K
- Sales cycle \< 9 months
- Win rate > 20%
- Pipeline coverage > 3x quota

### Use of Series A Funds ($5M-$10M)

**Sales & Marketing (50%):** $2.5M-$5M
- Scale sales team to 8-12 reps
- Marketing automation and demand generation
- Customer success expansion
- International expansion (LatAm)

**Product Development (25%):** $1.25M-$2.5M
- Advanced AI/ML capabilities
- Enterprise security and compliance features
- Platform scalability
